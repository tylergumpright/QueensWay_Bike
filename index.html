Bicycle Connectivity

The QueensWay is a proposed greenway/linear park along a 3.5 mile corridor of the now defunct Rockaway Beach Branch Commuter Line from Rego Park to Ozone Park.

The map below suggests possible bike lane routing alternatives between three strategic nodes along the QueensWay and one point of entry in Flushing Meadows Corona Park. PlaNYC states that by 2030 85% of New Yorkers should live within a 10 minute walk of a park. This metric of connectivity and open space could easily be adapted to cycling. This map aligns with community activists QNS.BIKE mission to target greater neighborhood connectivity between parks.
